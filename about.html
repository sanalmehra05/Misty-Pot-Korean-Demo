Our Story

Misty Pot was born from a desire to bring the vibrant, soulful energy of 1970s and 80s Seoul to the heart of Melbourne. Our philosophy is simple: authentic Korean flavors served in an environment that feels both nostalgic and refreshingly modern.

We take pride in our traditional stone plate grills and the "Misty Pot" – our iconic blue clay pot (ttukbaegi) that ensures every soup and hot pot stays perfectly steaming until the last bite.

Whether you're here for a quick lunch rice bowl or a long evening share-plate session with So-Maek, you're part of the Misty Pot family.
